A good designer should always keep eyes on the design in his or her daily life.

Looking at the designs in our daily life can help us develop the taste and ability to identify the pros and cons of designs. Therefore, we can In our first project, Good Design, Bad Design, we were asked to train ourselves to observe and think about the design of things around ours’ daily life.

Sketch and prototype on your idea

Rome wasn't built in a day. It is the same as your design. In order to make our ideas come true. Sketching and prototyping the idea in the early stage of the design process is vital. It not only can give you a basic idea on that your design is going to carry out but also can let you find out the deficiency in the early design stage so that you can revise and finalize your design.


In the project Design for others, we were asked to redesign the Technocipia website to target corporate groups. Before the final design, we made serval simple sketches on the paper and we used an app called Sketch to prototype the website on an iPhone screen. With the sketches and prototypes, we can see if our idea meets our design goals and expectations. Therefore, we can begin to implement our design.

Let users test on your designs

As a designer, it is sometimes hard to see from the user’s perspective because we tend to focus more on whether the page looks good and how to solve the technical problem in our design. So, we are likely to ignore what is the real world situation when the user uses our product. As a result, let users test our product and listening to their feedbacks can not only give us another perspective but also let us know what normal people interact with our product.


In the project Design for others, we created a chatbot that helps users determine which Operating System that users want to use for their next computer. The bot asks users series of questions like the usage of the computer and therefore narrow down the selection and gives users a recommendation. Observing how users interacting with our chatbot, we found some problems. For example, some users point out other aspects when they are choosing a new computer. Gathering the user’s ideas, we can better design our product
